🍕 The Origin Story

From Wood-Fired
to Getting Low

  • The Pizza Years — 2005–2019John ran the finest wood-fired pizza operation in Niagara. He stretched the dough by hand. He tended the oven with devotion. He called her "she." It was a whole thing.
  • The Terrible Tuesday — 2019The oven died. John held it as it cooled. He wept. He denies this. Everyone who was there confirms it.
  • The PivotThat same afternoon John picked up a pipe wrench, put on the sunglasses, removed the sleeves from his safety vest, and drove to his first pipe job. The pipe was perfect. He was smiling. He has been smiling ever since.
  • The AwakeningTurns out, John was always meant to lay pipe. The pizza years were simply the warm-up. He is now extremely good at getting big pipes into tight spaces and he finds the whole thing deeply satisfying.
